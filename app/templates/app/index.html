Our Aim

  • TO BE A FOUNDATION BASED ASSOCIATION. TO ASSIST POTENTIAL AND ACTUAL ENTREPRENEURS TO ACQUIRE, DEVELOP, IMPROVE AND SUSTAIN ENTREPRENEURIAL SKILLS.
  • TO PROTECT AND SEEK THE INTERESTS OF ENTREPRENEURS, AND TO ASSIST INTERESTED PERSONS IN ACQUIRING ENTREPRENEUR SKILLS.
  • TO RENDER ASSISTANCE WHETHER FINANCIAL OR MATERIAL TO ENTREPRENEURS, AND TO SERVE AS A RALLYING POINT FOR ENTREPRENEURS.
  • TO EMPOWER ENTREPRENEURS THROUGH WORKSHOPS, SEMINARS, AND ACQUISITION OF VOCATIONAL AND BUSINESS SKILLS.
  • TO HELP AND GIVE ASSISTANCE TO THE LESS PRIVILEGE AND THE NEEDY IN THE SOCIETY.
  • TO PARTNER WITH INDIVIDUALS, OTHER FOUNDATIONS, NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATIONS, OR GOVERNMENT AGENCIES, TO ACHIEVE THE ABOVE OBJECTIVES OR ANY OF THEM.

Our Mission:

We are a foundation-based association dedicated to supporting entrepreneurs at every stage of their journey. Our goal is to equip individuals with the skills, resources, and network needed to succeed in business and make a positive impact on society.
